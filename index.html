About Us

We are Angel in us

We are a non-charitable organsization mainly active for helping children in need. Our organization provides numerous facilities for children and also conducts various campaigns to help those who cannot afford basic human necessities. Angel in us takes responsibility of these young souls from educational expenses to their healthcare and also gives them a family to grow up with and share their life experiences.

Our Motive

This organization fight for uplifting children, and every other person who is suffering in any form. Moreover, the main motive of this associations is to establish a flourishing society with no odds and uncertainties. Children are provided shelter with every basic need. Helping this society to overcome all misfortunes by giving a helping hand is what our organization follows after.
